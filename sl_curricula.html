Programs of Study:

Schola Lingua offers complete curricula for students in the following langauge families. All students are welcome, but all students above 10 years of age must make a commitment to live on campus in order to fully immerse in the langauge. Semesters are four months and run year-long. All room & board is accounted for in tuition. Sixteen full semesters at Schola Lingua will render a student fluent in reading, writing, speaking, and listening in the target language. See the following programs of study for target language options.

Germanic Languages

Immerse yourself in the rich variety of German dialects, including Bavarian, Swabian, and of course Hochdeutsch. Schola Lingua also offers programs in Dutch, Afrikaans, Icelandic, and Norweigian. Advanced students may also take non-immersian courses focused on Old English, and the development of English (the most commonly spoken Germanic language on Earth).
