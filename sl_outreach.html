Foreign Service, Education, & Volunteer Opportunities

Schola Lingua recognizes that travel abroad is a major motivating factor for language learners. Schola Lingua encourages and fosters relationships overseas with like-minded schools around the world. These sibling schools offer tuition in various culturally and linguistically subjects of their homeland, and may at times offer volunteer roles or even employment opportunities for sufficiently competent students of Schola Lingua.

Manompana High School

High School Manompana lies on the northwest coast of Madagascar, a mile from the shores of the Baie de Titingue. HS Manompana serves a remote community of learners from rural and agricultural families. French is the spoken and taught language of the school as it is one of two official languages of the island. Schola Lingua students who wish travel to Manompana High School must demonstrate high proficiency in French and/or Malagasy.

Der Dialog Sprachcampus

Located in the heart of Vienna, Der Dialog offers comprehensive language education in German and English for high school students and adults. Der Dialog is a longtime partner of Schola Lingua and many of our respective staffs have shared teaching resources and students. Der Dialog Sprachcampus offers semester- or year-long courses, with volunteer and employment opportunities. Students of Schola Lingua who wish to study abroad at Der Dialog Sprachcampus must demonstrate proficiency in German (Hochdeutsch).
