Schola Lingua Grounds:

Schola Lingua is a destination educational school. The Schola Lingua grounds are very important to our model. Students gain experiential learning in the target language- this means students learn how to cook, craft, build, and create in the target language! It is normal to see our students cooking together in Italian, practicing equestrian care in Spanish, building a structure using German terms, or adventuring off-campus using Russian or Ukranian. Our grounds support this language education model and the technical education that comes with it. Students at Schola Lingua learn by using and living their target langauge.

Why Grounds are Important:

Schola Lingua is a destination educational school. Our Educational model is based on experiential immersion. An uninterrupted four years of study at Schola Lingua will result in fluency in the target langauge/s. We encourage welcome young learners but server learners of all ages.

Our staff includes of speakers of one or more of the target langauges in our programs of study. We teach target langauges through experiential and technical education, including agriculture, animal husbandry, the building arts, the fine arts, adventure & survival skills, and practical living skills.
