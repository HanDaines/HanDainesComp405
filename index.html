Who We Are:

Schola Lingua is a destination educational school. Our staff includes of speakers of one or more of the target langauges in our programs of study. Our speakers live, teach, and offer their technical skills on the grounds in a like-minded community of language-learners and accomplished polyglots. We teach target langauges through experiential and technical education, including agriculture, animal husbandry, the building arts, the fine arts, adventure & survival skills, and practical living skills.

What We Offer:

Schola Lingua is a destination educational school. Our Educational model is based on experiential immersion. An uninterrupted four years of study at Schola Lingua will result in fluency in the target langauge/s. We encourage welcome young learners but server learners of all ages.
